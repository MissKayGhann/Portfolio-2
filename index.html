About Me

Hiya, my name is Kareen and on the 16th of May 2022 I knew almost nothing about coding. I had learnt a little bit in order to get on the pre apprenticeship course at Generation UK but that was about it. 6 weeks later, I have been able to create my portfolio which you now see and some of the project that I have linked. I have come to love coding with all its never-ending challenges and the excitement of fixing problems, which I probably created myself - ha ha.

My desire is to become a Full Stack Developer through the apprenticeship route so that I can gain on the job experience as well as attaining a qualification.

In a few years’ time, I hope to have created a social media platform that will be able to give guidance on how to break into tech and to also become a mentor to those who are still finding their way. Its honestly social media and constantly speaking to those in the industry that gave me the confidence to get into tech with no knowledge.

Well, that’s a little about me.
